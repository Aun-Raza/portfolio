About me

Hello, my name is Aun Raza. I'm an ambitious programmer based in Toronto, Canada, set to graduate in December 2023. My passion lies in leveraging the power of code to solve complex problems and create innovative digital solutions.

Throughout my academic journey, I've developed a strong foundation in key programming languages and frameworks, which I'm excited to bring to a real-world setting. My aspiration is to continually grow my technical skills, taking them to new heights with each project I undertake.

Beyond coding, I understand the value of strong social networks in our industry. I'm eager to collaborate, learn from others, and contribute to a team working towards common objectives.
